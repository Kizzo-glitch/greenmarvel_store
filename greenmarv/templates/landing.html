Vitality Spray

Promotes hair growth, Prevents breakage and infections.

  • Ingredients:
    Active: Herbal Extract
    Essential oils: Rosemary; Vitamin E; Grapeseed; Cedarwood; Lavendar Oil; Castor Oil
    Preservative: Denatured Organic Alcohol


  • Benefits:
    Natural Ingredients: The use of herbal and organic ingredients reduces the risk of exposure to harmful chemicals, making it safer for both hair and scalp.

    Nourishment and Strengthening: Herbal extracts provide essential nutrients that help strengthen hair from the roots, reducing breakage and promoting healthier growth.

    Moisturizing Properties: Herbs and organic ingredients have natural moisturizing properties help maintain the hair's natural moisture balance, preventing dryness and frizz.

    Environmentally Friendly: This organic formulation is more environmentally friendly, reducing the ecological footprint due to the absence of synthetic chemicals.

    Improved Hair Texture: Regular use improves hair texture, making it softer, shinier, and more manageable without the heavy buildup associated with conventional hair sprays.

    Natural Hold and Styling: Provides a natural hold that keeps hairstyles in place without making the hair stiff or sticky, allowing for flexible and touchable styles.

    Reduced Allergic Reactions: The absence of synthetic fragrances and harsh chemicals can reduce the likelihood of allergic reactions, making it suitable for sensitive skin and scalps.

    Antioxidant Protection: Contains antioxidants that protect hair from environmental damage, such as UV rays and pollution.

    Pleasant Natural Fragrance: The natural fragrance from herbs and essential oils provide a pleasant, subtle scent without the overpowering artificial smell of conventional hair sprays.

    Infection Protection and Eradication: Anti-bacterial and antifungal properties of the essential oils and organic alcohol effectively remove all sorts of infections.
