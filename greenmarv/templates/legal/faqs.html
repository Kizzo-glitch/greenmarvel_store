About Marvelously Green

Marvelously Green is the consumer brand of Green Marvel (Pty) Ltd, a South African organic hair care company. We were founded in 2023 — and started trading commercially in 2024. The company is 100% Black-owned, 67% female-owned, and youth-owned.

We're guided by the spirit of Botho (Ubuntu) — the philosophy of humanity, community, and oneness with nature. Every formulation combines traditional African botanical knowledge with modern pharmaceutical science, giving you products with centuries of proven efficacy and modern quality assurance.

Our operations are based at 620 Park Street, Arcadia, Pretoria, Gauteng. From here we handle product manufacturing, fulfilment, and customer service for orders across all nine South African provinces.

Three things set us apart:

  • Pharmaceutical-grade science. Our founders are pharmaceutical scientists. Every product is formulated under good manufacturing practice (GMP) standards with batch-by-batch stability and testing.
  • Rooted in traditional knowledge. Each formulation uses plant ingredients with documented centuries of use in African hair and scalp care. We combine that heritage with modern testing methods to verify safety and efficacy.
  • No harsh chemicals. Free from parabens, sulphates, synthetic fragrances, and other ingredients commonly found in mass-market hair care that can cause irritation, hair loss, or scalp damage.

Yes — every product is formulated, blended, and packaged right here in South Africa. We're proud to be part of the country's growing community of local manufacturers offering world-class quality.

Ingredients & Safety

Our products are built around plant-based botanical extracts and natural carrier oils, with no synthetic actives or harsh chemicals. Full ingredient lists are printed on every product label and available on each product page on our website.

What you won't find in our products:

  • Parabens (linked to hormone disruption)
  • Sulphates (strip natural oils, cause irritation)
  • Synthetic fragrances (common allergen)
  • Formaldehyde-releasing preservatives
  • Mineral oils and petroleum derivatives
  • Artificial colourants

Yes — our products are 100% vegan (no animal-derived ingredients) and cruelty-free (never tested on animals). Stability and safety testing is conducted exclusively in laboratory settings using approved scientific methods.

Our formulations use mild, natural ingredients and are generally considered safe for use during pregnancy and breastfeeding. That said, we always recommend you consult your obstetrician or healthcare provider before introducing any new product during pregnancy.

Always check the full ingredient list on the product label before use. If you have known plant or oil allergies, look for the relevant ingredients. We strongly recommend a patch test before first use: apply a small amount to the inside of your wrist or behind your ear, leave for 24 hours, and discontinue if any redness, itching, or irritation occurs.

If you experience an unexpected reaction, please stop use immediately and contact us at admin@greenmarvel.co.za.

Our packaging is recyclable. We're continuously working on reducing our environmental footprint — from using locally-sourced raw materials wherever possible to minimising packaging waste. Sustainability is one of our core operating values.

Payment

We process all payments securely through Payfast, South Africa's leading payment gateway. You can pay using:

  • Visa and Mastercard (credit & debit)
  • Instant EFT (Capitec Pay, FNB, Standard Bank, ABSA, Nedbank, and other SA banks)
  • Bank transfer (manual EFT — order is held until payment reflects)
  • Masterpass and Snapscan (in some cases)

Yes — completely. Your card details and banking credentials are entered directly on Payfast's secure servers, not on our website. Payfast is PCI DSS Level 1 certified (the highest security standard for payment processors) and uses 256-bit SSL encryption. We never see, store, or have access to your full card number or CVV.

Your payment method is charged immediately when you complete checkout. We then process and dispatch your order. If for any reason we're unable to fulfil your order (e.g. out of stock), you'll be refunded in full within 5 working days.

We don't currently offer instalment options, but it's something we're exploring for the future. For now, all orders are paid in a single transaction at checkout.

If a payment fails, no money is taken from your account. Common causes are insufficient funds, an expired card, incorrect CVV, or your bank's fraud protection blocking the transaction. Try the following:

  1. Check that your card details are entered correctly
  2. Try a different payment method (instant EFT often works when card fails)
  3. Contact your bank if they're blocking the transaction
  4. If you've been charged but didn't get a confirmation email, contact us — we'll resolve it immediately

Returns & Refunds

For health and hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns of opened or used products. We accept returns of unopened, sealed products within 7 working days of delivery for a full refund.

If you received the wrong item, a damaged item, or an item with a manufacturing defect, please contact us immediately and we'll arrange a replacement or refund at our expense.

While extremely rare with our natural formulations, adverse reactions can happen with any cosmetic product. If you experience irritation, please stop using the product immediately and contact us at admin@greenmarvel.co.za. We'll work with you to understand what happened and may offer a refund or replacement with an alternative formulation depending on the circumstances.

Once a refund is approved, the funds will reflect in your account within 5–10 working days. The exact timing depends on your bank and the payment method used. EFT refunds are typically faster than card refunds.

For returns due to a change of mind or order error on your side, the customer covers the return shipping cost. For returns due to our error (wrong item, damaged in transit, manufacturing defect), we cover return shipping in full.
