About cookies on our site

We use cookies to keep your cart working, remember your login, and understand how visitors use our site. Here's the full breakdown.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device on subsequent visits and provide a better browsing experience.

Cookies are essential for many modern web features, such as:

  • Keeping you logged in as you navigate between pages
  • Remembering items in your shopping cart
  • Saving your preferences (e.g., language, currency)
  • Helping us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it

4. Managing Your Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

4.1 Browser Settings

Most browsers let you control cookies through their settings. You can usually find these in your browser's "Privacy" or "Security" section:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

4.2 Opt-Out Tools

  • Google Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Do Not Track: Enable Do Not Track in your browser settings (we respect this signal)
⚠️ Important: Disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent the website from functioning properly — your cart won't work, you can't log in, and checkout will fail.
