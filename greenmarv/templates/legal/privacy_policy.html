Your privacy matters to us

This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Green Marvel (Pty) Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA) and other applicable South African data protection laws.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit greenmarvel.co.za, create an account, or purchase our Products.

We are the Responsible Party for the processing of your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: Username, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile information: Full name, billing address, shipping address, phone number
  • Order information: Products purchased, order history, delivery preferences
  • Communications: Messages you send us via email, contact forms, or SMS

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, links clicked, products viewed
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, device type, screen size
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see our Cookie Policy)

3.3 Information From Third Parties

  • Payfast: Payment confirmation status (we do not receive your card details)
  • The Courier Guy: Delivery tracking information
  • Google Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics
💳 Payment data: We never see or store your full credit card information. All payments are processed by Payfast on their secure servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

3.1 To Fulfill Your Orders

  • Process payments through Payfast
  • Arrange delivery via The Courier Guy
  • Send order confirmation and shipping notifications via SMS
  • Manage returns and refunds

3.2 To Manage Your Account

  • Authenticate you when you log in
  • Send password reset emails
  • Display your order history
  • Pre-fill checkout forms with saved information

3.3 To Improve Our Service

  • Analyze website usage to improve user experience
  • Identify popular Products and optimize our offering
  • Fix technical issues and bugs

3.4 To Communicate With You

  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send marketing communications (only if you opt in)
  • Notify you of important changes to our service

3.5 Legal and Compliance

  • Comply with legal obligations under South African law
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the following parties, and only as necessary:

💳

Payfast

Payment processing only. They receive your billing details to process the transaction.

🚚

The Courier Guy

Receives your shipping address and contact details to deliver your order.

📱

SMSPortal

Sends order confirmation SMS using your phone number.

☁️

Railway / Cloud Hosting

Hosts our website and stores data securely.

We may also disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights or the safety of others.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Our security measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages, including login and checkout
  • Encrypted password storage — we never see your plaintext password
  • Secure payment processing via Payfast (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Limited access — only authorized staff can access customer data
  • Regular security updates to our software and systems

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., SARS requires invoices to be kept for 5 years)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

When information is no longer required, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

7. Your Rights Under POPIA

Under POPIA, you have the following rights:

👁️
Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

✏️
Right to Correction

Request that we update inaccurate or incomplete information.

🗑️
Right to Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements).

🚫
Right to Object

Object to direct marketing communications at any time.

📤
Right to Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

⚖️
Right to Complain

Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator if you believe we have not complied with POPIA.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at admin@greenmarvel.co.za. We will respond within 30 days.

📮 Information Regulator (South Africa)
Website: inforegulator.org.za
Email: complaints.IR@justice.gov.za
